### Now let's speak straight facts, do you mind?
Simone is a firm advocate of human art against the abuse of that _sloppery slop_ "AI art". His stance does not come
unmotivated. Besides the obvious negative impact that AI Slop spreadout has had on human artists, that usage of AI is a matter of concern for countless reasons. One above all - **if shortage of ram was not enough for you!** - negative climatic impact. 

### I believe it is important to address the elephant in the room
In this world of ours is kinda difficult to discern between good and evil, innit? \
\
AI can be a good tool, an assistance in certain - tedious - tasks. But it should never substitute human work in terms of creativity. As an example I do like AI assisted coding as far as I don't feel too biased toward a design pattern or something like that. 

### The elephants may actually be two
Although it is not an easy task to distinguish good from bad, it is absolutely certain that companies are abusing AI Slop cause it costs less than paying artists. But AI Slop is soulless, it would be fun to create memes with if only does not waste this much energy. I can condone the use of it as inspirational model, a mockup tool or as a corrective tool on Graphic Design and Illustration programs, but I believe that **art belongs to artists**.
